I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at the University of Alberta, Canada. I currently work in deep learning, Bayesian nonparametric methods, machine learning, fairness, data privacy, generative models, and information-theoretic methods. I also serve as an Assistant Lecturer at the University of Alberta. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in Artificial Intelligence in the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science at the University of Hong Kong. These roles and research areas are reflected in my CV. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2} :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

My research interests include Bayesian analysis, Bayesian nonparametric methods, model checking and hypothesis testing, entropy and information theory, multivariate analysis, machine and deep learning, fairness and data privacy, generative models, adversarial neural networks, and variational inference. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
